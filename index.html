About Us

Established over 40 years ago, Jay Gopal Dairy Mart has been a beloved local destination for quality dairy products and snacks. We pride ourselves on offering fresh, high-quality goods to our customers.

Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has made us a staple in the community. We source our ingredients locally whenever possible and adhere to strict quality standards to ensure that you receive only the best products.

Our Products

We offer a variety of products made from the finest ingredients to ensure quality and taste. Each product is crafted with care, using traditional methods to maintain authenticity.

Sweets

Delicious Indian sweets made from the finest ingredients, including Gulab Jamun, Ladoo, and Barfi. Perfect for celebrations or as a sweet treat any time!

Snacks

Crunchy and savory snacks perfect for any occasion. Our Farsan range includes Samosas, Kachoris, and Bhujia, ideal for tea time or gatherings.

Milk

Fresh, pure milk sourced from local dairies. We ensure our milk is free from additives and is perfect for cooking, baking, or enjoying a glass chilled.

Ice Cream

Rich and creamy ice creams available in various flavors, including Mango, Pistachio, and Vanilla. Made with natural ingredients for a delightful taste.

Cold Drinks

Refreshing cold drinks to quench your thirst. From traditional Lemonade to modern Soft Drinks, we have something for everyone!

Curd

Freshly made curd, perfect for your meals. Our curd is smooth, creamy, and packed with flavor, ideal for side dishes or desserts.

Chaas

Traditional buttermilk to keep you cool in the summer. Made with spices and herbs for a refreshing taste.
